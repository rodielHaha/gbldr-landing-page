How it works

We designed and tested prototypes that helped identify pain points
in automating the process. Here are the steps on how it works:

1. Client posts a project/job

The client submits a job or project listing that includes essential information such as location, duration, and budget.

4. Selected freelancer provides a detailed qoutation.

Upon being hired for the job, the freelancer gives a comprehensive cost estimate for the project.

2. Freelancers send bid proposal.

Freelancers submit their bid proposals along with an approximate cost estimate for the project.

5. Project starts with progress billing

The client makes a down payment for the project and the remaining amount is billed through progress billing. The progress billing system is integrated into the website to promote transparency for the client.

3. The client selects the best rated professional.

Client selects the best freelancer based on profile and past work after reviewing all applications.

6. Client rates the freelancer.

Upon completion, the client evaluates the freelancer's performance. The project information can then be included in the freelancer's profile, confirmed by the client.
